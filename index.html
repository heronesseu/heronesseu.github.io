Driving!

The Philippines will not give everyone the best experience in driving. Uneven roads, heavy traffic, limited parking spaces, and high prices of gasoline are just some of the reasons why driving is not enjoyable in the country. I am often told that my love of driving will fade out once I experience being a driver for a long time. I learned how to drive in 2021, and I still love driving up to this day.

I also wondered why that is. It is not that I love being stuck in the traffic. I also hate that gasoline prices are sky-high. I hate driving, especially when I have to look for parking. And I find the uneven roads annoying too. But why do I still love driving? Upon pondering, it is not the convenience that I like about it. I actually prefer commuting than finding parking spaces. What I like about it is the peace that it gives me. Long trips or short, I like how my mind quiets down as soon as I hold the steering wheel. Since I have to be present at all times, I have no choice but to focus on driving. As a person who is always anxious, it feels like a vacation or an espace from my thoughts.
